Remarks

Queue name must consist only of lowercase letters, digits and underscores, other characters aren't permitted. Some examples:

  • "critical" (good)
  • "worker_1" (good)
  • "documents queue" (bad, whitespace)
  • "MyQueue" (bad, capital letters)
